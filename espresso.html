How to Make Espresso

  1. Grind your coffee beans of choice on a fine grind.
  2. Fill group handle with grounds and tamp down the contents.
  3. Place group handle in group head and set the espresso to brew.
  4. Shots should pull for approximately 25 seconds, resulting in dark colored espresso and a thin line of pale brown crema at the top.
  5. Note: Espresso is best enjoyed within 2-3 minutes of the shot being pulled. After this time frame, the shot will "die" (if not already added to some sort of espresso drink), resulting in a slightly ashy taste.
  6. Enjoy!
