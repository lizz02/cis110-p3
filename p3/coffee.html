How to Make Coffee

  1. Grind your coffee beans of choice on medium grind.
  2. Heat just over 12oz of water (filtered is recommended).
  3. Place a coffee filter over your glass. Soak the filter with hot water and empty any excess from your mug.
  4. Measure 3 tablespoons of grounds into the filter.
  5. Pour enough water so that the grounds are wet and then allow it to drain for a few seconds. Then, pour the remaining water into the filter. This should create a blooming effect, causing a light brown foam to emerge from the grounds after the water is added.
  6. Wait a few minutes for all the water to drain through the filter into your mug.
  7. Enjoy!
