How to Make A Cappuccino

  1. Pour around 8oz of milk into a pitcher.
  2. Submerge the steam wand completely in the milk before turning the wand on.
  3. Angle the pitcher so that the content begin to swirl and begin to aerate the milk, carefully pulling the pitcher back at an angle until a tearing sound can be heard. Aerate for roughly 6-8 seconds.
  4. Continue to steam milk until it is at your preferred temperature. When finished, tap the pitcher gently against the counter so the foam settles.
  5. Pull a shot of espresso. While, the shot is pulling, add any sweetener you would prefer to your mug.
  6. When the shot has finished pouring, add the contents to your mug, swirling slightly to mix in any sweetener.
  7. Pour milk and continue swirling to ensure the contents are thoroughly mixed.
  8. Clean steam wand, wiping it down and turning it back on for a moment to dispose of any residual milk.
  9. Note: Depending how strong you prefer your espresso drinks, the milk/espresso ratio will vary.
  10. Enjoy!
